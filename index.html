About Me

Hi! I'm Sai Raghavi, a passionate full-stack web developer with a keen interest in building dynamic and responsive web applications. My journey into the world of web development began during my time at Mahindra University, where I completed my bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the 2020-2024 batch.

During my academic years, I developed a fascination for coding and problem-solving, which led me to explore various programming languages and frameworks. I particularly found my passion in full-stack web development, where I love creating seamless user experiences from both the frontend and backend.

I also had the opportunity to intern as a frontend developer for 6 months, where I worked extensively with Angular to build user interfaces that are intuitive and efficient. This hands-on experience solidified my understanding of modern web technologies and enhanced my ability to contribute to full-stack projects.

As a developer, I enjoy taking on new challenges, learning new technologies, and continuously refining my skills. Whether it's working with frameworks like React and Angular or diving into backend development, I strive to stay on top of the latest trends in the tech world. My goal is to keep growing in this field and contribute to building innovative solutions that can make a difference.
